The total salary expenditure per department.

**Current value:**

Marketing: 388,000

## KPI 2: Projects Without Assignments

**Definition:**  
Count of projects that currently have no employees assigned.

**Current value:**  
Blockchain Pilot: 0 employees  
Quantum Computing Research: 0 employees

**Interpretation:**  
Highlights projects at risk due to lack of staffing. Helps management address resource allocation before delays occur.

## KPI 3: Above-Average Departments

**Definition:**  
Departments where the average salary exceeds the company-wide average salary.

**Current value:**  
Engineering: 78,167  
Research: 69,750  
Marketing: 64,667  
Finance: 68,833

**Interpretation:**  
Shows departments with higher-than-average salaries. Helps in workforce planning and compensation review.

## KPI 4: Employee Project Load

**Definition:**  
Average number of projects assigned per employee.

**Current value:**  
Average: 2.7 projects per employee

**Interpretation:**  
Measures employee workload and engagement. High value indicates well-utilized staff; low value may indicate underutilization.
